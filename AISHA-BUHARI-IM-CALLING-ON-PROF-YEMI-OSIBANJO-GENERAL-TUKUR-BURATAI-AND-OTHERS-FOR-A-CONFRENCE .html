AISHA BUHARI- I'M CALLING ON PROF. YEMI OSIBANJO, GENERAL TUKUR BURATAI AND OTHERS FOR A CONFERENCE

Source: SAHARIGIST

27 May, 2020

I am calling on Prof. Yemi Osibanjo, General Tukur Buratai, and others for a conference, it's time to make Nigeria Great again - Aisha Buhari Tells Officials.

The Nigerian first lady, Aisha Halilu Buhari has called for a meeting conference with the Nigerian political stakeholders in the governmental position to resolve matters arising in the country.

The Nigerian First Lady who made the statement public on 25/5/2020, while announcing to the government officials including the Nigerian Vice President, Yemi Osibanjo, Chief General of Army, Tukur Buratai, and others about the security breach in the country.

Saharagist reported it was stated that during the lockdown period, which had been imposed by the Nigeria-elected President Muhammadu Buhari to assist in curbing the spread of the novel virus in Nigeria.

While speaking, Aisha Buhari also informed the people that the government officials will work effectively to ensure that the lives and property of the Nigerian citizens are safe.

While making a further statement, she said;

"The security breach in the country is becoming worse and worse every day, it's time to rejuvenate and make Nigeria great again, therefore, I will be calling on a meeting conference with the Nigerian government officials including the Chief Security forces and Vice President to deliberate on the matters arising in the country."

After this statement by the first lady of Nigeria; Nigerians have reacted saying that the call by the first lady is necessary given the security condition of Nigeria. since the demise of Abba Kyari, the former Chief of Staff to the Federal government of Nigeria. Therefore, it is very crucial that Nigerians and their leaders consolidate to reach discuss on workable strategies to make Nigeria great again.

Nigerians have witnessed too much before the pandemic, and the current pandemic ravaging citizens, taking lives on a daily basis is even intensifying the pains of the already-injured country people.

We can recall that since the demise of the former chief of Staff, Abba kyari, the first lady of Nigeria has been at the forefront, representing in a high official position before the newly appointed Chief of Staff to the president assumed office.

Wuku Clara Nengimote
