NYSC RELEASES DATE FOR THE PASSING OUT OF 2019 BATCH B STREAM 1 CORPS MEMBERS

Source: Opera.com

16 May, 2020

According to a circular released on Friday May 15, 2020, it was revealed that the winding up of 2019 Batch 'B' Stream I Corps Members is scheduled to hold on Thursday 28th May, 2020 and all corps members are to submit their final clearance to their Local Government Inspectors (LGIs) from their employers.

The exercise is to be carried out with all the associated activities, culminating in the Passing-out parade. All the States, except Adamawa, Borno and Yobe, are to prepare for the entire ramifications of the Winding-up/Passing-out programme.

Local Government Inspectors (LGIs) are to ensure that the employers of the passing out corps members sign and stamp their clearance and State Coordinators are requested to establish contact with Security Agencies at the Local Government level for observance of the required protocol in order to ensure a hitch-free exercise, taking cognizance of the prevailing circumstances.

Corps Members who are currently not on their places of primary assignment (PPA) due to COVID-19 must submit final clearance before their certificates can be issued to them upon return to their states of deployment.

The Distribution of Certificates of National Service be staggered for a period of ten (10) days, at the fist instance, in compliance with the approved protocol of social distancing, and continued thereafter depending on the number of the remaining Certificates.

Each distribution point in the State should not have more than thirty (30) Corps Members, with a minimum of three 131 NYSC officials supervising the process.

State Coordinators should engage officers on GL 12 and above to monitor the distribution of Certificates. Such officers are to ensure that social distancing is strictly adhered to at the various Local Government Areas.

I sincerely wish all the Batch 'B' Stream I Corps Members that will be passing out on Thursday, 28th May 2020 favour in life and congratulations in advance.!
