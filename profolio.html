Content Production

Since 2017, O'buchi Multimedia has being part of film productions. Over time, our team has built a client base with Multinational and National organisations and offer professional Photography, Videography, Graphics design and Website Creation services.

We run a weekly programme titled 'YOUR TAKE- THE POWER SECTOR AND YOU' created to educate Nigerians on electricity distribution through engaging conversations with Electricity Consultants, Stakeholders and Electricity Consumers aired on our YouTube Channel.

With the involvement of the Institute of Family and Works Integration (IWFI) and the Creative Film Project (CFP), a short film called 'Eleven 59' was produced to advocate for parental attention in the face of access to digital technologies and against cyber-bullying/harassment. Eleven 59 was nominated on an International Short Film Festival.

We also produce a Teenagers Talk show series called Today’s Teens with Trish which has grown from online to TV viewing. Check out our YouTube channel for more.

Project Management / Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

We are committed to CSR initiatives and are actively involved in projects that empower individuals and contribute to National Growth.

O’BUCHI MULTIMEDIA has strategically managed Non Governmental Organisations through project development, management, budgeting and implementation.

With our customer service expertise, we have been able to create and foster innovative relationships between our Clients and Private/National parastatals.

Working with Favour’s Open Arms Foundation, a Non-Governmental Organisation (NGO), we have been able to develop, plan and execute about 18 philanthropic outreaches and national projects in the sub region and outside. This has enabled us work with organisations like INEC, Connected Development (CODE), YAIGA Africa, CISLAC, SOBCA and others.
